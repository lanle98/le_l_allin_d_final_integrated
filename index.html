Website Promo

Vietnamese food is known for its distinct use of fresh, fragrant and aromatic flavours. There is a balance of sweet and sour, spicy and cooling, fresh and salty flavours. In this website NGON, we put our effort to introduce and also help you to have more knowledge about one of the most well-known countries for cuisine in the world - Vietnamese cuisine.

Website Products

Goi Xoai
( Mango Salad )

Vietnamese mango salad (Goi Xoai) is a colourful dish featuring green mangoes, shrimp, and fresh herbs like mint, thai basil and cilantro. It’s a mouth-watering combination of sweet, sour, and salty with lots of contrasts in texture.

Bun Thit Nuong
( BBQ Chicken Noodle )

Bun Thit Nuong is a Vietnamese rice-vermicelli noodle dish topped with grilled pork, fresh herbs like basil and mint, fresh beansprout salad, and chả giò (Spring Rolls). The dish is dressed in nước mam fish sauce (Nuoc Mam).

Com Tam
( Broken Rice )

Com Tam is broken rice with grilled pork, shredded pork, pork skin, fried egg, pork meatloaf, pickled carrot, and sliced cucumber. It tastes salty and sweet, driving your taste buds wild with each bite.

Pho

Pho is a Vietnamese soup consisting of broth, rice noodles called Banh Pho, a few herbs, and meat, primarily made with either beef (Pho Bo) or chicken (Pho Ga).
